PICTOREAL is a group of high-spirited and effervescent individuals. It is one of the most sought-after non-technical clubs at Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT). It has published 24 vibrant magazines so far, all of them portraying the remarkable contributions of its members.

TEDXPICT

TEDxPICT is a group of proactive individuals focused on providing a platform to bring various stakeholders together to share ideas, stories, and innovation. In the context of the current fast-developing world, sharing ideas and experiences is very much necessary for overall societal progress. TEDxPICT plays a vital role in spreading these essential things to the masses.
